About Us

LightHouse foundation is a Non-Profit Organisation with the aim of spreading the love of God.

Welcome to LightHouse Foundation

LightHouse foundation is a Non-govermental organisation which was established in October 2019 with the aim of reaching out to more less priviledged and orphanage in the local society and beyond and it has since then kicked off with passionate team members and supporters from all walks of Life with the aim of coming together to reach more people in order to spread around the love of God through the vehnicle of love and kindness.

Support With Cash

Your cash can go a long way, that's why we the lighthouse foundation give opportunity and arrangement for those who will like to be part of this great work towards making our society a better place to do so through voluntary contribution in cash in order to have access to gather more resources with the hope satisfying and making those who didn't have access to life to do so with joy and happiness.

Giftings

We the lighthouse foundations cherish those who through gifts are determined to show their act of love towards the less priviledged and the orphanage. your gifts can indeed go a longer way and also make room for others to partake of your kind gesture towards humanity

voluntary

The quest towards making the world a better place to be it's not possible without the comng together of like minds and great people with great ideas who can join forces together in making what seems impossible possible spreading love and changing the world starts with change in mindset individually, then collectively. The Lighthouse foundation looks forward to receiving volunteers towards the betterment of our society.
